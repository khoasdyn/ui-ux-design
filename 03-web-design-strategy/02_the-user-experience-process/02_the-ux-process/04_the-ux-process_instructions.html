Usability in the Physical World vs. on the Web

Here’s an interesting comparison of the physical world with the digital world by the usability expert Jacob Nilsen. In the video, Nilsen argues that usability guidelines are essential in web design because users have many alternative sites just a click away, unlike in the physical world where they are more likely to tolerate inconveniences.
