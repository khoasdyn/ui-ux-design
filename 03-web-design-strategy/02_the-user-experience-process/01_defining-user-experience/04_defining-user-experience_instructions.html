Laws of UX

This is a beautifully design website explaining a number of UX laws and best practices that designers can consider when building user interfaces.

https://lawsofux.com/

Check out these three, which focus on the psychology of humans:

  • Paradox of the Active User: Humans will just start using software instead of reading a manual first

    • https://lawsofux.com/paradox-of-the-active-user/

  • Peak-End Rule: People remember an experience largely by how they felt at its peak and at its end

    • https://lawsofux.com/peak-end-rule/

  • Selective Attention: People filter out information that isn’t relevant and focus their attention on what’s related to their goals

    • https://lawsofux.com/selective-attention/
