Here are some user and client needs for the Pasadena Conservatory. Recall that the users of the conservatory include current and prospective students and their parents, as well as music patrons. Can you think of some possible content and functionality requirements to meet these needs?

User Needs

  • How can I support the Conservatory?

Client Needs

  • Increase enrollment

  • Easy way to update the website

  • Facilitate ticket purchases
