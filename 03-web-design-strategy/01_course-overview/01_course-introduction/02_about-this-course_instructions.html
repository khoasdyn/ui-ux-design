Web Design: Strategy and Information Architecture is Course 3 of 4 in the UI/UX Design Specialization. To read more about the other courses in this Specialization please visit https://www.coursera.org/specializations/ui-ux-design.

As reiterated in the video, this course and the one that follows, Web Design: Wireframes and Prototypes , are considered to work together as a pair within the larger sequence of the Specialization. You will need to complete this course first as you will be continuing the project started here in the fourth and final course.

These two courses are also considered to be at an intermediate level, as the amount of work ramps up from week to week, especially in Course 4. It takes a lot of time to plan and build a website properly, so budget your time accordingly. I assure you the results will be fruitful if you put in the work!
