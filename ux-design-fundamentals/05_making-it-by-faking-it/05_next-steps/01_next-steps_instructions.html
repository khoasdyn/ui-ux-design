Next Steps

If you’ve finished this course, I encourage you to build on your UX skills by enrolling in the next two courses offered in the UI/UX Specialization , Web Design: Strategy and Information Architecture and Web Design: Wireframes to Prototypes , taught by my colleague Roman Jaster. In these two courses, you will further hone your UX skillset by delving into user research and understanding your target audience, setting goals and defining strategy, establishing scope and structure, and finally developing a compelling look and feel. You’ll apply these skills to a single, large-scale project: developing a comprehensive plan for a responsive website which you will develop over two courses.

Good luck with your studies!
