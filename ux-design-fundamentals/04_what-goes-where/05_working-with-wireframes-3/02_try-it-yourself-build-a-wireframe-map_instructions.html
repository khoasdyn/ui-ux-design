Try it yourself: Build a wireframe map

In this week's assignment, you built a set of wireframes.

While I didn't require that you turn a wireframe map in for that assignment, it'll be helpful for you to create a wireframe map now that you've built your wireframes.

Once you've done so, lay them out and experiment with the arrangement and organization of your map.

Play around with grouping your screens by type, such as interaction or topic.

What do you notice?

Remember that a wireframe map can help you build better interactions.

Do you spot any inconsistencies among your screens? Make changes to your wireframes accordingly.
