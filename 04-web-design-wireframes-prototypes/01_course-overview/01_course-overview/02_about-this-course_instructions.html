Web Design: Wireframes to Prototypes is the last course in the UI/UX Design Specialization. To read more about the other courses in this Specialization please visit https://www.coursera.org/specializations/ui-ux-design

As reiterated in the video, this course and the one that precedes it, Web Design: Strategy and Information Architecture , are considered to work together as a pair within the larger sequence of the Specialization. Please be sure you have completed the Strategy and Information Architecture course before you begin this one—you will be continuing the project you started in that course in this one.

These two courses are considered to be at an intermediate level, as the amount of work ramps up from week to week, especially here. It takes a lot of time to plan and design a website properly, so budget your time accordingly. I assure you the results will be fruitful if you put in the work!
