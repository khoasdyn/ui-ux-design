There are four required assignments (quizzes) in this course. You will need to achieve the minimum passing percentage for each assignment in order to pass the course.

In addition to the weekly assignments, there is an optional peer reviewed project. I highly recommend you work on the project and submit your work to your peers for feedback to develop the skills I teach in this course.

  • Week 1 Assignment: Wireframes

  • Week 2 Assignment: Home Page Mockups

  • Week 3 Assignment: Full Set of Visual Mockups

  • Week 4 Assignment: Mockup Revisions

Throughout the course you also will find short, optional activities which I encourage you to complete. Some of these are short quizzes, either standalone or embedded within a video, or discussion prompts. Others, titled "Try it yourself", are exercises meant to be completed "off-platform". They are not required, but completing all of these activities will help you practice the concepts I’m teaching and complete your assignment at the end of the week.
