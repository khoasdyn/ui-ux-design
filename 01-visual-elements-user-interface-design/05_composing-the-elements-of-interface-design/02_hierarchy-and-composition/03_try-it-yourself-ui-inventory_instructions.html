Similar to the exercise in the previous week, examine the user interface of a digital product or service that has both a web-based version and an app version. Ask yourself:

  • Are there any actions or interactions you can do in one version that you can’t in the other? Why do you think that’s the case?

  • How does the navigation change? Do you find one easier to navigate?

  • How does the content change between the web and the app? Why do you think that’s so?

Some examples are Google Maps, Instagram, web-based email clients (i.e. Gmail), Spotify, or even Coursera.

Spend no more than 20 minutes on this activity, and record your observations.
