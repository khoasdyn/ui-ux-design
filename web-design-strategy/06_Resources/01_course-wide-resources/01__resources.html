UI/UX tools and software

Figma

https://www.figma.com/

One application does it all: User research documentation, slide decks, wireframes, mockups, and clickable prototypes

Whimsical

https://whimsical.com

A dedicated software tool for creating wireframes. It’s much more limited in its features, which is actually helpful since you’ll be forced to thing about structure and not visual presentation.

UX Research

any of the tools offered by Optimal Workshop : Treejack, Chalkmark, OptimalSort, Questions, and Reframer

Sitemap tools

I recommend Adobe Illustrator , which allows you complete flexibility in designing your sitemap. But there are dozens of tools—some free—available specifically for this purpose.

Web resources for UI/UX

A List Apart : A website for people who make websites.

Shop Talk Show : An internet radio show about the internet starring Dave Rupert and Chris Coyier.

Suggested Books/Reading

Garrett, Jesse James. The Elements of User Experience: User-Centered Design for the Web and Beyond . New Riders, 2011.
