Using the search engine of your choice, find the websites of at least two other restaurants. These restaurants do not have to be delivery-only places, but should offer the same or similar food or drink as your restaurant.

Once you’ve found the restaurant websites, go through each website and consider the following questions:

  • What is something you think the website does well?

  • What is something you think the website could improve upon?

  • Are there any design elements in use that you might incorporate in your own site?

Spend no more than 15 minutes on this activity.
