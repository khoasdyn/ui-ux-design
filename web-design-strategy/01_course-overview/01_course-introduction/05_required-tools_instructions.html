It probably goes without saying, but I'll reiterate here: while you can watch the lecture videos and complete quizzes on a mobile device, to complete the assignments you will need access to a desktop or laptop computer with the appropriate software installed (see below). You can't really do graphic design work properly on a smartphone or tablet.

For most of this course we'll be focusing on the planning stages of your proposed website, namely strategy and scope. To complete the first three assignments in this course you will need word processing software —such as Word, Google Docs, or InDesign—whatever works best for you to write out and present your ideas clearly. Your assignment submissions will be submitted as PDF documents so you should be able to export whatever you write to that format.

For the last assignment you will be required to draw a sitemap. I recommend Adobe Illustrator , which I use in my sitemaps, but any software that will let you easily draw a bunch of rectangles and lines will do. Use whatever software works best for you, your workflow, and your budget.
