There are four required assignments (quizzes) for each week in this course. You will need to achieve a minimum grade of 80%  in each assignment in order to pass the course.

In addition to the weekly assignments, there is an optional peer reviewed project. I highly recommend you work on the project and submit your work to your peers for feedback to develop the skills I teach in this course.

  • Week 1 Peer Review: Project Ideas

  • Week 2 Peer Review: Strategy

  • Week 3 Peer Review: Outline of Scope

  • Week 4 Peer Review: Sitemap

Throughout the course you will also find short, optional activities which I encourage you to complete. Some of these are short quizzes, either standalone or embedded within a video, or discussion prompts. Others, titled "Try it yourself", are exercises meant to be completed "off-platform". They are not required, but completing all of these activities will help you complete your project at the end of the week.
