If you’ve finished this course, I encourage you to build on your UI design skills by enrolling in the next one offered in the UI/UX Specialization , UX Design Fundamentals. In that course, you will learn how content is organized and structured to create an experience for a user, and what role the designer plays in creating and shaping a user’s experience. You will be led through a condensed process that acts as a roadmap for developing robust UI/UX design: from ideation and sitemapping, to the creation of paper and digital prototypes. You will also be led through the development of a digital prototype for a multi-screen app of your own invention—a slightly more advanced project, but still suitable for a beginner.

We hope to see you in the next course.
