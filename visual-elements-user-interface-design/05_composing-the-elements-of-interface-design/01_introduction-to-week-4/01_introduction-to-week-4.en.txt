[MUSIC] Welcome to the fourth and final week
of visual elements of interface design. This week, we're going to take our
individual interface components and see what happens when we try to put them
together into a more complex structure. We'll be looking at how to get our
components to work harmoniously as a family. Figuring out how hierarchy works in the
interface and discussing conventions and expectations of contemporary
interface design. We'll also be examining how to navigate to
different screens and how to build visual relationships between different kinds
of content but within a single sign. Finally, we'll be discussing
different platforms, how to create variable content for
different screen sizes, and looking at how to organize complex bodies
of content into user friendly structures.
