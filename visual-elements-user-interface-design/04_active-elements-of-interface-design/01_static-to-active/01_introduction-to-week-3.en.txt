This week, we're going to take our static interface elements and begin to think about how a user interacts with them. In other words, how to bring these elements to stage closer to having a life on the screen. We'll be looking at navigational conventions such as menus, buttons and icons in different states. Our focus will move from what the graphic interface looks like to include how it works, how it responds to the user. By adding interactivity to our static designs, we'll begin to think more deeply about the role that designer plays in shaping a user's interactive experience.
