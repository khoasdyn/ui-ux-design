It probably goes without saying, but I'll reiterate here: while you can watch the lecture videos and complete quizzes on a mobile device, to complete the assignments you will need access to a desktop or laptop computer with the appropriate software installed (see below). You can't really do graphic design work properly on a smartphone or tablet.

In all of the assignments in this course we will be creating fairly complex graphics (wireframes and visual mockups) for the proposed website you began to develop in Web Design: Strategy and Information Architecture .

For creating wireframes, I recommend Whimsical or Figma .

For creating visual mockups, I strongly recommend Figma , which is the industry standard.
